How to Use Intentioned

  1. Select a scenario from the sidebar to set the conversation context.
  2. Choose your input mode: Voice Activity (auto-detects speech) or Push-to-Talk (click to record)
  3. Click the Start button to begin the session.
  4. Enable Webcam for eye contact tracking (optional).
  5. Speak naturally - the AI will respond in real-time.
  6. End the session to receive your detailed analysis report.
Tip: Keep eye contact with the camera for best results. Speak clearly and avoid long pauses.

  • Multiple training scenarios
